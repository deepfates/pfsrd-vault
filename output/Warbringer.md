Name: [[Warbringer]]
Aura: strong enchantment
CL: 14
Slot: item
Price: 61,375 gp

Description: This massive, wickedly curved red-bladed sword is a +3 keen vicious falchion that murmurs a low, rumbling battle chant when drawn.
