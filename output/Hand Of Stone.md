Name: [[Hand Of Stone]]
Aura: moderate divination
CL: 7
Slot: neck
Price: 27,000 gp
Weight: 2 lbs.
Description: This mummified dwarf hand hangs on a steel chain around a character's neck, bringing the wearer more in tune with the earth beneath her feet. If the wearer spends a standard action concentrating on the hand, she gains tremorsense out to a range of 30 feet until the start of her next turn. This ability only allows her to sense creatures and objects that are in contact with earth or stone.
