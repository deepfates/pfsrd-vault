Description: When wrapped around a wearer's waist, this belt grants a +6 enhancement bonus to Dexterity. Treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn. The wearer can remain incorporeal for up to 10 rounds per day. The rounds need not be consecutive.
