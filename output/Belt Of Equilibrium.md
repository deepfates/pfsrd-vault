Name: [[Belt Of Equilibrium]]
Aura: faint conjuration

Description: As long as this belt's wearer doesn't move more than 5 feet during her turn, she ignores the penalties for being fatigued, shaken, or sickened until the end of her next turn or until she moves more than 5 feet, whichever occurs first.
