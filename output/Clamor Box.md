Name: [[Clamor Box]]
Aura: moderate illusion
CL: 10
Slot: item
Price: 2,000 gp
Weight: 2 lbs.
Description: This delicate tin music box can produce a great volume of noise at a specific time determined by its owner. Four command words determine what sorts of sounds it is to play (battle, fire, massacre, or riot), and a fifth determines the delay (anywhere from 1 round to 20 minutes) until the box begins to produce the desired sound. The noise from the box is as loud as 40 people. The sound persists for 2d6 rounds.
