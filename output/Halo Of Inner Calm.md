Name: [[Halo Of Inner Calm]]
Aura: strong abjuration
CL: 15
Slot: head
Price: 16,000 gp
Weight: 1 lb.
Description: This silvery ring looks like a halo. When worn by a tiefling it hovers just above his head, though it still uses the head magic item slot. It helps to calm the baser emotions and the dark whispers that plague tieflings, granting the wearer a +4 resistance bonus on saving throws against all spells with the emotion descriptor. When worn by a tiefling of a good alignment, it provides spell resistance 13 against spells with the evil descriptor and a +2 sacred bonus on saving throws.
Requirements: [[Craft Wondrous Item]], holy aura, creator must be a tiefling of good alignment
