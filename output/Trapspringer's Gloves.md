Name: [[Trapspringer's Gloves]]
Aura: faint divination
CL: 5
Slot: hands
Price: 4,000 gp
Weight: 1 lb.
Description: These studded leather gloves look similar to workman's gloves, though they are reinforced with numerous metal strips that the user can extend or retract as she wills, turning the tip of each finger into a customizable tool useful in examining and disarming traps. While worn, these gloves grant a +5 competence bonus on all Disable Device checks made to disarm traps. In addition, the gloves grant the user a +1 luck bonus on all saving throws made against traps.
Requirements: [[Craft Wondrous Item]], find traps
